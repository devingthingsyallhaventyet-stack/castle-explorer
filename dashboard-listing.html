Timeline
Key historical events — shown on History tab
c. 900 BC
Iron Age Fort
Archaeological evidence shows human occupation of Castle Rock dating to the late Bronze Age.
1130s
Royal Castle Established
David I held his court at the castle and his mother Saint Margaret's Chapel was built, the oldest surviving building in Edinburgh.
St Margaret's Chapel
📷 Kim Traynor · CC BY-SA 3.0
1314
Recaptured by the Scots
Thomas Randolph, Earl of Moray, scaled the castle walls with 30 men and recaptured it from English forces.
1573
Lang Siege
The castle was bombarded in a siege lasting almost two years, the longest in its history.
1927
Scottish National War Memorial
The memorial was opened within the castle, designed by Sir Robert Lorimer.
