About

A great red brick tower keep built by Ralph Cromwell, Lord Treasurer of England. Its six storeys with elaborate Gothic fireplaces represent the finest medieval brickwork in England.

Tattershall Castle is a castle in Tattershall, Lincolnshire, England. Since 1925 it has been in the care of the National Trust.
