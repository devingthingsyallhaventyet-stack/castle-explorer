About

Home of the Parsons family since 1620, Birr Castle is famous for its historic science centre and Great Telescope. Its 120-acre demesne gardens hold over 1,000 species of trees and shrubs.

Birr Castle is a castle in the town of Birr in County Offaly, Ireland.
