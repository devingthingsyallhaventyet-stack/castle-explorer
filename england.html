The Castles of England

England's landscape is stitched together by its castles. From the white cliffs of Kent where Norman keeps still stand sentinel, to the wild Northumbrian coast where Bamburgh rises from volcanic rock — every county holds its own chapter in this ancient story. These are not just ruins. They are the bones of a kingdom, still standing after a thousand years of wind, war, and weather. Walk their halls and you walk through time itself.
