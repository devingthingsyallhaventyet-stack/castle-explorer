Stirling Castle
The Great Hall was restored in 2011 and is incredible — the hammer-beam roof is one of the finest in Britain. Also, the castle kitchens have been recreated to show a royal feast being prepared, which most visitors don't realise is there. Worth a look.

Tintern Abbey
The car park fills up quickly on weekends — arrive before 10am or park at the village car park (5 min walk). Also, the best photos are from the field across the river, not from inside the ruins.

Trim Castle
You can only access the keep with a guided tour (runs every hour). The grounds are free to walk around but the interior tour is €5 and absolutely worth it — the guide was brilliant and full of stories about the filming of Braveheart here.
